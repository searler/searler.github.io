--- layout: post title: Couchdb thoughts from Dallas Techfest categories: - CouchDB tags: [] status: publish type: post published: true meta: _wp_old_slug: '' --- The Dallas TechFest had an interesting presentation on CouchDB by Scott Davis. Most of the attendees expressed various degrees of shock, horror and disbelief! The concepts of CouchDB are certainly very unusual for developers with a SQL background. Those concepts are much more familiar to Lotus Notes developers and it might be useful to revisit the design patterns used in creating Notes applications.
